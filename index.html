How To Accessorize Your New Toyota Tacoma

If you just rolled a brand-new Toyota Tacoma into your driveway, you have miles of enjoyment lying ahead of you. The Tacoma is one of the most versatile trucks on the market, and it can be fully accessorized to suit your needs. Whether you’re looking for aftermarket upgrades or vinyl decals, Taco Vinyl is your go-to source for everything “Toyota.” Today we’re here to share four ways you can start customizing your new vehicle — read on to discover what’s possible when your imagination is the limit!

Storage Solutions

Whether you’re going fishing, camping, or hiking, you’ll need to equip your new Tacoma with storage accessories that can safely haul all your gear. A bed liner or cargo net can keep your belongings from sliding around, while a tonneau cover can protect them from the elements. For large items like bikes and kayaks, check out our roof racks and bed racks that are custom-designed for Tacomas!

Safety Accessories

Safety should always be a top priority, and this is no less true when it comes to adventuring in your new truck. Whether you’re on the road or off the map, Taco Vinyl has several safety accessories that should be must-haves for your Tacoma. For starters, make sure you have an all-in-one first aid kit at the ready. Additionally, our vehicle recovery gear can help you out in sketchy situations.

Vehicle Body Armor

We’re guessing you didn’t buy a Tacoma just to take it to the grocery store and back. If you’re like us, you can’t wait to take your truck off-road and start blazing trails. However, before you get started, you should consider equipping your Tacoma with vehicle body armor. This can help protect your truck from bumps and scrapes on the most rugged terrain.

Lighting Kits

Aftermarket lighting kits can help you see and be seen, whether you're in the woods or the open road. A set of off-road lights can help you navigate rough areas, while LED taillights can make you more visible to other drivers.

No matter how you use your Tacoma, there are plenty of ways to customize it. And with the right accessories from Taco Vinyl, your new Toyota will be ready for any adventure!
